AUTOBIOGRAPHY

My name is Matthew Steven Toledo, nickname: Steven. I was raised and spent most of my life in Muntinlupa, and I went to De La Salle Zobel for elementary and high school. Eventually, I was accepted into the BS Statistics program in the University of the Philippines Los Banos, but I soon shifted to the university's BS Computer Science program.

That leads to now, where this website is made as part of a requirement for CMSC 100, a course included in the BS Computer Science curriculum.

Anyway, here's some trivia about me...
